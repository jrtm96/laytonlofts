About Us

We have just finished construction of these brand new Loft-style apartments! 2 minutes to 13th Street and 3 mins away from I-41, I-43, I-894 and I-94! Food restaurants will surround you! 5 minutes away from 27th Street, 10 minutes away from the nearest Walmart and the Airport is just 5 minutes away! Boasting an Attached/Detached Garage -Including outside parking in the Parking lot, you'll live comfortably in a quiet and safe neighborhood! Washer and Dryer, Stove, Refrigerator and Kitchen Island Marble-Tops are part of every unit! New Kitchen cabinets with high grade wood! And for your storage needs you'll find storage closets in every unit. We have a Tenant portal to simplify your paying, communication and tracking needs. Land-owner pays water for your convenience!
